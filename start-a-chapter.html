Start a Chapter

Want to launch The Interwoven Project at your university? Great! Chapters are designed to be lightweight, repeatable, and student-run.

What you’ll do as a chapter

  • Recruit and train volunteers using beginner-friendly materials
  • Run recurring sessions with a community partner
  • Keep the experience welcoming and accessible
  • Track participation and improve over time

What we provide

  • A chapter launch checklist and session format
  • Beginner teaching guides and pattern suggestions
  • Templates for recruiting, scheduling, and outreach
  • Ongoing support as you get set up
Note: We’re not gatekeeping. We just want to make sure chapters start in a way that’s sustainable.
