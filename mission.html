Our mission

The Interwoven Project supports the cognitive, social, and emotional well-being of older adults through consistent, relationship-centered engagement. We partner with senior living communities to run beginner-friendly crochet sessions that encourage focus, conversation, and a sense of purpose. Our work prioritizes dignity, patience, and genuine human connection.

What problem we’re addressing

Many older adults experience limited opportunities for meaningful cognitive stimulation and sustained social interaction, particularly in institutional settings. Activities may exist, but they are often passive, inconsistent, or isolating. We address this by providing regular, low-pressure engagement that promotes active participation and shared attention.

Why Crochet?

Crochet offers gentle cognitive engagement through repetition and sequencing while allowing participants to work at their own pace. The tactile, rhythmic nature of the activity creates a calm environment that supports conversation, memory sharing, and emotional comfort.

When appropriate, completed items may be donated or gifted, allowing participants to contribute to others and experience a tangible sense of purpose beyond the activity itself. The craft serves as a tool for connection rather than an end goal.

What we do

We run recurring crochet sessions with older adults and train student volunteers to teach patiently and adapt to individual needs. Sessions are designed to promote cognitive engagement, social connection, and emotional well-being through hands-on activity and consistent interaction. When appropriate, participants may choose to donate or gift completed items, but participation is never contingent on completion or output.

Core values

Consistency

Showing up regularly matters more than running “perfect” events.

Accessibility

Beginner-first materials, clear instruction, and low-pressure participation.

Respect

We meet people where they are and prioritize dignity and choice.

Why chapters matter

Chapters let the model scale without losing the human part. Each chapter adapts to its university’s structure and local partnerships, while following shared standards for safety, consistency, and quality.
